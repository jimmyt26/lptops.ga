Efficient, Affordable, Practical Laptops

Not everyone can afford a top-of-the-line, $3,000 Gaming PC that can run games without breaking - on the flip side, not everyone can afford a $5,000, Retina Display MacBook just so they can work without their computer exploding. So what makes the perfect laptop? Well, we've organized an assortment of Gaming Laptops, Casual Laptops, and just some regular laptops for the people that just need to get some work done.

Gaming Laptops

Starting us off, we have the ASUS ROG Zephyrus GX501

Pros: Not only does this laptop have a 15.6” Full-HD Screen and have a GTX 1070, but it's quite the budget laptop as well. Coming in at around $1,995 and is ultra thin, quiet, and keeps cool. This is definitely recommended for beginners looking for an inexpensive alternative to a wallet-weeping AlienWare laptop.

Cons: You may want to note that this laptop doesn't have many upgradable options and doesn't have the keyboard, however you can always replace that for a low cost.

Next up, the MacBook's enemy, the Razer Blade

Pros: This laptop can handle 4k gameplay and has Nvidia GeForce GTX 1060 (6GB GDDR5 VRAM) graphics with 16 GB of Ram. It's definitely worth the low price of around $1,799. It has impressive battery life and a 14 inch screen, with 256 GB of storage. That's enough space for a couple GTA Vs and at least 20 Minecrafts.

Cons: This computer isn't exactly stealthy as it whirs loudly when playing with its full potential, however this is avoidable. It also has limited upgrade options.

Third on the gaming list is the Gigabyte SabrePro 15

Pros: This laptop happens to be the cheapest laptop on our list, coming in at around $1,340. This machine takes gaming to another level, in fact a much more affordable one. It has a Nvidia GeForce GTX 1060 with a Intel Core i7, and even comes with a touchpad if that's what you're into.

Cons: This computer doesn't have the greatest battery life, but then again, who's playing Fortnite without an outlet nearby? It also has underwhelming speakers but external ones are cheap and easy to find.
